Big Bend Park

Brewster, Texas

There is a place in Far West Texas where night skies are dark as coal and rivers carve temple-like canyons in ancient limestone. Here, at the end of the road, hundreds of bird species take refuge in a solitary mountain range surrounded by weather-beaten desert.

Barnwell Mountains

Gilmer, Texas

Barnwell Mountain Recreation Area Loop is a 10 mile lightly trafficked loop trail located near Gilmer, Texas that offers scenic views and is rated as moderate. The trail offers a number of activity options.

Good Water Loop

Georgetown, Texas

Good Water Loop is a 26.9 mile heavily trafficked loop trail located near Georgetown, Texas that features a lake and is rated as moderate. The trail offers a number of activity options and is accessible year-round. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash.

Hueco Tanks

El Paso, Texas

Formed thirty four million years ago when magma entered an older limestone formation and then cooled, the Hueco geology emerged as weather eroded away the limestone and carved the odd sculptural hoodoos seen today.

Enchanted Rock

Fredericksburg, Texas

The massive pink granite dome rising above Central Texas has drawn people for thousands of years. The scenery, rock formations and legends are magical, too! Enjoy hiking, backpacking, rock climbing, camping, and stargazing. Enchanted Rock offers 11 miles of trails.

Padre Island

Corpus Christi, Texas

Padre Island National Seashore separates the Gulf of Mexico from the Laguna Madre, one of a few hypersaline lagoons in the world. The park protects 70 miles of coastline, dunes, prairies, and wind tidal flats teeming with life.
